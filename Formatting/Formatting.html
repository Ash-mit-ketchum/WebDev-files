My Locality

My name is Ashmit and the Locality I live in is Ramphall Chowk. It is based in Dwarka. Ramphall chowk has a small Giant Market. There are dozens of shops here. It is also connected to the rest of delhi via 2 metro lines. There is 24/7 electricity available with water as well. There are also a lot of government provided facilities available as well, which include a sewer system, and a park .
